Listen Live!

Live Since 1971

WJSV 90.5 FM, Morristown High School's very own radio station. We've been broadcasting JSV (Just Solid Vibes) for over 50 years. Tune in anytime for music and radio shows, entirely selected and hosted by MHS students. Listen over the radio on 90.5 FM anywhere in the Morristown area, or over the internet using our web player.
